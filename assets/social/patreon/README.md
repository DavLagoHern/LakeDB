## Paid tiers

### LakeDB Supporter

- Price: EUR 5/month
- Free trial: disabled
- Member limit: none
- Shipping address: not required

Description:

> Support the continued development of LakeDB and help fund builds, code
> signing, release infrastructure and the journey toward LakeDB 1.0. LakeDB
> remains the same application for everyone. This membership is a way to
> support the project and follow its development more closely.

Benefits:

- Monthly supporter development update
- Early previews of upcoming features and interface changes
- Supporter roadmap polls
- Behind-the-scenes development notes
- Optional acknowledgement as a LakeDB supporter
- Everything published to free members

### LakeDB Mega Supporter

- Price: EUR 15/month
- Free trial: disabled
- Member limit: none
- Shipping address: not required

Description:

> Give LakeDB a higher level of voluntary support and help cover the ongoing
> cost of builds, signing, distribution and development. Mega Supporters
> receive the same LakeDB application as everyone else and all Supporter
> updates, with optional recognition for their additional contribution.

Benefits:

- Everything included in LakeDB Supporter
- Optional Mega Supporter acknowledgement
- Occasional group roadmap discussion
- A higher level of direct support for independent LakeDB development

The paid tiers must not promise exclusive LakeDB features, priority technical
support or individual meetings. This keeps the membership useful and
sustainable without creating a separate paid edition of the product.
